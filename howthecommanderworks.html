How the Commander works

Here i will explain how the commander of my deck, Grothama, all devouring, works. So when Grothama enters the battlefield, every other creature on the battlefield gains the ability to fight Grothama.

When anyone declares attackers, the player declaring attackers may have those attackers fight Grothama first outside of combat(meaning keywords that are combat keywords will not work such as trample or double-strike) and then they attack the person they declared to fight if those creatures survive fighting Grothama.

When Grothama leaves the battlefield, the people who dealt damage to it will draw equal to the amount of damage they dealt to Grothama.
